Menopause

It is normal and natural. There is a common misconception that the change begins a time of inevitable and prolonged discomfort and declining quality of life. The reality is that menopause only marks the end of child bearing years. Supporting the body in its ability to manage a smooth hormonal transition and replenishing its energy reserves is important to a continuously active lifestyle.

In harmony with that goal, there are specific vitamin, mineral and special nutritional factors that can be taken. Dr. Vactor can develop a program for you so you can look and feel your best naturally. Dr. Vactor is here to help, so please don't hesitate to call.
