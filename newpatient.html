New Patient Experience

All around the world, people are realizing that Chiropractic care is a natural and safer way of obtaining and keeping good health. Chiropractic care is a conservative, drug-free approach to better health that has helped millions of people since 1895.

First Step: Free Consultation

The first step to feeling healthy is to schedule your Free Consultation and explore the benefits of chiropractic care. During this consultation, the doctor will discuss your overall health, previous medical history and the reason you are visiting, as well as your diet, sleeping patterns, work environment, and exercise routine. Together, the two of you will discuss what options are available to you and how you can achieve optimal health.

The Free Consultation is a great way for you to discover the benefits that chiropractic care can provide for you and your family. In addition, before anything is done, the cost of exam and/ or x-rays is discussed.

Please fill out the Case History Form before your first visit to the Wexford Chiropractic Centre.

Second Step: The Examination

Your first examination will allow us to gain a complete understanding of your condition. This helps to ascertain the tissues involved. After all, pain can come from muscles, joints, ligaments, nerves, etc. Determining the source of the problem helps the Doctor to develop a plan of care. After the exam, x-rays are taken when needed to provide us with a picture of your sPine and even limited soft tissue structure. On occasion, depending on your injury, other advanced testing may be required to insure that your condition is properly diagnosed.

Third Step: The Health Care Report

On your return visit, you and Dr. Vactor will sit down and discuss his evaluation from your initial exam. We will determine if you are in need of chiropractic care or a referral to another healthcare specialist. If we determine that chiropractic care is an option, we will then discuss what can be done about your problem, how long we expect treatment to last in order to meet your goals and how much treatment will cost.
