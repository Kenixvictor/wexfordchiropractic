Directions to the Wexford Chiropractic Centre

From I-79/T-Bones: Exit 73 toward WEXFORD. Go 2.1 Miles to Wexford Chiropractic Centre on the right hand side
(130 Wexford Bayne Rd.) If you go to the stop sign, you've gone too far.

From Rt. 19 South: Go 1 mile past the Wexford Plaza (which is on the right) and make a right at the traffic light onto North Chapel Drive. (Billco Motors on the right). go .25 miles to the stop sign and make a another right onto Rt. 910 - Wexford Bayne Rd. Go up to the stop sign at Church Road and proceed through to the 3rd building on the left - 130 Wexford Bayne Rd. Wexford Chiropractic Centre.

From Rt. 19 North (Cranberry): At Northway Christian Community Church (on the right) and Sheetz (on the left) – go .6 miles and make a right at the stop light onto Church Rd. (at an angle) past St. Alphonsus Church. Go to the stop sign and make a right. Wexford Chiropractic is the 3rd building on the left – 130 Wexford Bayne Rd.

From Gibsonia (Rt 8): go straight on Rt. 910 - Wexford Bayne Rd. for 6.7 miles. At Church Rd, pass through the stop sign and Wexford Chiropractic is 3rd building on the left – 130 Wexford Bayne Rd.
